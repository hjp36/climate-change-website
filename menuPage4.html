Arctic Circle's Melting Glaciers

Learn More
How to Help!
  • Warming temperatures caused by the burning of greenhouse gases
  • Melts glaciers at rapid speeds because average temperatures are now higher
  • Melting ice also contributes to rising sea levels
  • Destroys habitats for many species within the Arctic Circle
  • Try to carpool with people or ride a bike, it releases less emissions into the air
  • Shut off lights and appliances when not in use
  • Use energy star appliances (Approved appliances that use less energy)
